PRIVACY POLICY

We respect your privacy and your right to control who has access to your personal information. However, Fashism is designed to allow users to post photographs for public review and comment. Accordingly, by submitting your photograph and/or posting comments, you waive any privacy expectations you have with respect to those photographs and/or comments and to our use thereof. If you do not wish to have your picture or comments viewed by or disclosed to others, do not use our website.

We may collect certain other personal information from you that we do not post on our website. We do not intentionally transfer this information to unaffiliated third parties without your consent. However, we reserve the right to transfer such information without your consent in response to a court order or subpoena or as otherwise required by law. Also, we may use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website and to share with advertisers. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of men and Y number of women visited our website, but we would not disclose anything that could be used to identify those individuals.

When you enter Fashism, we collect your browser type and IP address. In addition, we store certain information from your browser using "cookies." A cookie is one or more pieces of data stored on your computer that provides information about you. We use session ID cookies to confirm that you are logged in. These cookies terminate once you close the browser. By default, we use a persistent cookie that stores your login ID (but not your password) to make it easier for you to login when you come back to Fashism. You can remove or block this cookie using the settings in your browser if you want to disable this convenience feature.
